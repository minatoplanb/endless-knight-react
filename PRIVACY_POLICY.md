# Privacy Policy for Endless Knight

**Last Updated:** February 3, 2026

## Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Endless Knight mobile game (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by the developer (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service.

## Information Collection and Use

The Application does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personal information from its users.

### What We Don't Collect

- ❌ Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Device identifiers
- ❌ Usage analytics
- ❌ Crash reports
- ❌ Any other personal data

### Local Data Storage

The Application stores game progress data **locally on your device only**. This includes:

- ✅ Game save data (player stats, inventory, achievements, etc.)
- ✅ Game settings and preferences

This data:
- Remains on your device
- Is never transmitted to our servers
- Is never shared with third parties
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the Application

## Third-Party Services

The Application does not use any third-party services that collect information used to identify you.

**Note:** If we add features in the future (such as advertisements or in-app purchases), this privacy policy will be updated accordingly, and you will be notified.

## Children's Privacy

The Application is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

## Security

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no risk of your data being accessed or shared without authorization.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
